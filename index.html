The Mission

CleanClick aims to restore digital autonomy to surgeons. While operating rooms have advanced, interacting with digital data remains a bottleneck.

Our solution utilizes surface Electromyography (sEMG) to translate micro-muscle impulses into digital commands, keeping surgeons in full control.
