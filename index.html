The Mission

CleanClick restores digital autonomy to surgeons. Operating rooms are advanced, but interacting with digital data still breaks workflow.

We use sEMG signals to translate micro-muscle movements into commands — eliminating communication delays and keeping surgeons in control.

Project Timeline

Phase 1 — Problem Discovery

Mapped surgical workflow friction and identified control bottlenecks.

Phase 2 — Signal Exploration

Tested sEMG signals through surgical gowns and evaluated reliability.

Phase 3 — Interaction Design

Designed gesture-based control and clutch logic.

Phase 4 — Prototype Build

Integrated sensors with low-latency interface.

Phase 5 — Testing

Simulated OR testing and refinement.
