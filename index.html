Design Thinking Process

From clinical observation to breakthrough innovation

1

Problem Discovery

Clinical shadowing revealed surgeons breaking sterility or waiting for nurses to control displays. Communication lag disrupts surgical flow.

2

Ideation

Evaluated voice control (ambient noise) and eye tracking (line-of-sight issues). sEMG emerged as the optimal solution for sub-gestural, fabric-penetrating control.

3

Anatomical Mapping

Identified optimal sensor placement on forearm flexors. Tested signal penetration through surgical gown fabric layers for reliable detection.

4

System Architecture

Designed clutch-gate algorithm to distinguish surgical tool grip from control gestures. Bluetooth HID ensures universal compatibility with existing OR systems.

5

Prototyping & Testing

Built functional MVP with Myoware 2.0 sensors and ESP32. Validated signal integrity through surgical gown fabric. Currently optimizing gesture library.

6

Impact Vision

Transforming surgical workflow through seamless human-computer interaction. Direct control eliminates communication barriers and maintains sterility throughout procedures.

How It Works

Sub-gestural control through three revolutionary stages

Input: Muscle Signal

Forearm sEMG sensors detect micro-muscle contractions in the flexor carpi radialis. Sub-gestural movements invisible to the naked eye, completely imperceptible under surgical gowns.

Processing: Clutch-Gate Logic

Proprietary algorithm filters ambient noise and surgical tool grip signals. The "clutch-gate" ensures zero false positives when holding instruments, activating only during intentional gestures.

Output: Display Control

Bluetooth HID output navigates CT/MRI scans, zooms imaging, and controls OR monitors. Instant, sterile, autonomous—without breaking surgical focus or asking for assistance.

Core Technology

Medical-grade innovation for the modern operating room

⚡

Sub-Gestural Control

No large movements required. Imperceptible muscle contractions detected through fabric, preserving sterility and surgical precision.

🛡️

Clutch-Gate Logic

Intelligent signal processing prevents "ghost clicks" during surgical tool manipulation. Activates only during deliberate control gestures.

👁️

Zero Line-of-Sight

Unlike camera-based systems, CleanClick works perfectly even when staff block the view. Direct muscle interface, zero optical dependencies.
