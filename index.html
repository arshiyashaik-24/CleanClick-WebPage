How It Works

Sub-gestural control through three revolutionary stages

Input: Muscle Signal

Forearm sEMG sensors detect micro-muscle contractions in the flexor carpi radialis. Sub-gestural movements invisible to the naked eye, completely imperceptible under surgical gowns.

Processing: Clutch-Gate Logic

Proprietary algorithm filters ambient noise and surgical tool grip signals. The "clutch-gate" ensures zero false positives when holding instruments, activating only during intentional gestures.

Output: Display Control

Bluetooth HID output navigates CT/MRI scans, zooms imaging, and controls OR monitors. Instant, sterile, autonomous—without breaking surgical focus or asking for assistance.
